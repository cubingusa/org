Rubik's WCA North American Championship 2024

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA will be hosting the Rubik's WCA North American Championship 2024 on July 18-21, 2024 at the Minneapolis Convention Center in Minneapolis, Minnesota!

This competition will be the WCA's second North American Championship, and the seventeenth US National Championship. Each year, CubingUSA hosts a major competition bringing together hundreds of the top American speedcubers. We are pleased to welcome competitors from other North American countries, and the rest of the world, for the first North American Championship held in the US.

This competition is brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community.
