Travel Information

Venue

The competition will be held at the Salt Palace Convention Center in Salt Lake City, Utah. We're pleased to be working with the Salt Palace, which will be a great venue for our competition.
Address: 100 S W Temple, Salt Lake City, UT 84101
Main Room: Exhibit Hall 2 and 3
Long Events: Meeting Room 155 A/B

Hotel

(Updated 6/26/2018!) We have reserved a new block of rooms at the Salt Lake Marriott Downtown at City Creek, conveniently located near the competition venue. Our original block of rooms at the Radisson Hotel Salt Lake City Downtown has filled. Please book using the reservation link provided to ensure the best rate.
Marriott Address: 75 S W Temple, Salt Lake City, UT 84101
Radisson Address: 215 W S Temple, Salt Lake City, UT 84101
Reservation Link: Marriott

Transportation

Nearest Airport: Salt Lake City International (SLC) is eight miles from the competition venue.
Public transit: UTA TRAX is the easiest public transit option for getting to and from the airport. The Green Line runs directly to the competition venue and hotel. Trains run about every fifteen minutes and cost $2.50 per ride.
