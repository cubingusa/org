Rubik's x TheCubicle CubingUSA All-Stars 2025

CubingUSA is excited to bring you a special event: a two-day, 17-event, all-star National Championship! This competition will give the top solvers across the country the opportunity to vie for the title of US National Champion.

We're taking a one-year break from our usual CubingUSA Nationals format to bring you an exciting weekend of championship-tier cubing. We hope you'll join us in person or on the stream to watch the top solvers in the country!
