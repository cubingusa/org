WCA World Championship 2025

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA will be hosting the WCA World Championship 2025 on July 3-6, 2025 at the Seattle Convention Center in Seattle, Washington! This will be the twelfth Rubik's Cube World Championships, and the first in the USA since 2013.

We'll be sharing more information about the competition over the next few months. We plan to open registration in Fall 2024.
