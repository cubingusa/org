Rubik's WCA World Championship 2025

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA will be hosting the Rubik's WCA World Championship 2025 on July 3-6, 2025 at the Seattle Convention Center in Seattle, Washington!

This competition will be the twelfth World Championship, and the seventeenth US National Championship. We are pleased to welcome competitors from the entire global speedcubing community to the third World Championship held in the US.

This competition is brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community.
