CubingUSA is giving up our usual National Championship in 2025 in order to organize Rubik's WCA World Championship 2025 for the global community; we gladly welcome international competitors at either Rubik's WCA World Championship 2025 or CubingUSA Nationals 2026 instead. International competitors will not be featured on stage, will not be eligible for awards or prizes, and will not be shown on the livestream. {% endcall %} {% call faq.faq_item("Will this format be used for CubingUSA Nationals 2026?", "2026") %} No. CubingUSA Nationals 2026 will return to our typical convention-style competition format. {% endcall %} {% call faq.faq_item("How does advancement work?", "advancement") %} The top 8 US competitors will advance to the finals of each event, in addition to any international competitors who place ahead of the 8th US competitor.
