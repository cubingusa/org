Apply to be on Staff!

Thanks for your interest! We are looking for applications for Staff Team, Data Team, Help Desk, and Live Stream Commentators; you can read more about each role below. Staff applications will open in December 2024. Staff will be accepted on a rolling basis. Please apply early for full consideration.
Signing up to be on staff is a commitment; please make sure to let us know as soon as possible if you will not be able to attend. We will need a final confirmation of your attendance no later than May 14, 2025.

Staff Team

Staff Team members will have judging, running, and scrambling assignments throughout the competition. Expect to spend about 2/3 of the competition volunteering, and the rest either on breaks or competing. Delegates on the Staff Team will be able to apply to be a Stage Lead.

Data Team

Data Team members will be in charge of data entry, double checking scorecards, and printing subsequent round scorecards. Expect to spend about 2/3 of the competition volunteering, and the rest either on breaks or competing.

Help Desk

We're looking for parents and other volunteers to work around 5 hours per day at Registration, answering questions about our event and checking competitors in, and helping with some other tasks like shopping runs. This is a great job for parents who'd like to help out and keep busy during the competition!

Live Stream Commentators

Live Stream Commentators will work with our Live Stream team to provide commentary for the competition live stream. Applications for this position will close on January 15.

Perks

Staff members will receive the following perks:
  • Refunded registration fee (after the competition)
  • Lunch for each day of the competition (specifics to be determined)
  • Staff t-shirts
  • Travel support -- one of:
    • Accommodation in a staff hotel room (18+ only)
    • $40 per night reimbursement for your own hotel room
    • $60 per night reimbursement for your own hotel room with no refunded registration (great option for parents staffing)
    • Daily parking reimbursement (great option for local cubers)
  • One free spectator ticket
