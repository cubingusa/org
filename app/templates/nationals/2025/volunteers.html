We are looking for volunteers to help us run CubingUSA All-Star Championship 2025! We have a few different roles that we are looking to fill:

  • Core Volunteers: The main judging, running, and scrambling roles for the competition.
  • Data Entry: The team responsible for entering all times into WCA Live.
  • Live Stream Commentators: The team providing expert commentary for the viewers at home watching the stream. People in this role do not need to be on-location in Providence.

Volunteers will receive free registration (if applicable), free entrance to the competition, and provided lunch. In addition, we have a limited budget to support travel expenses; please indicate in your application whether you'll need financial support in order to participate as a volunteer.

Applications for the volunteer team will open by April 1.
