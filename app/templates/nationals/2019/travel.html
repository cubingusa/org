Transportation

Nearest Airport: Baltimore-Washington Airport (BWI) is ten miles from the competition venue. The easiest way to reach the city from BWI is either car or train.
Train: Amtrak Northeast Regional and Acela Express trains serve both BWI Airport and Baltimore. Baltimore Penn Station is 1.6 miles from the venue.
