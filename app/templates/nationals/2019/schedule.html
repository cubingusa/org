CubingUSA Nationals 2019 Schedule

Competitors must check in before their first event. Check-in will be open starting at 9:00 AM on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday. You do not need to check in on Thursday if you do not have any events on Thursday. Registration is in the Otterbein Lobby via the Sharp St entrance.

Competitors in 3x3 Multi-Blind must submit their cubes for scrambling by 10:30 AM on the day of the attempt.

All times are Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) (UTC-4).
