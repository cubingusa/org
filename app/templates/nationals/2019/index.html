CubingUSA Nationals 2019

We're pleased to announce that Rubik's Presents CubingUSA Nationals 2019 will be held August 1-4, 2019 at the Baltimore Convention Center in Baltimore, Maryland!

CubingUSA Nationals 2019 will be the 15th US National Championship. Each year, the competition brings together hundreds of the top American speedcubers, as well as many international competitors, for a fun weekend of solving puzzles.

Thanks to Rubik's Brand Ltd. for their generous sponsorship, and to TheCubicle for providing the prize pool. See prize details below.

Nationals is the largest and most professional speedcubing competition in the USA each year, brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community.

(CubingUSA is a 501(c)(3) registered non-profit organization, and we are looking for volunteer Board Members and Software Developers. If you're interested in working with us, please email: positions@cubingusa.org)

Registration

Registration will take place on the WCA website. There are qualifying times in all events; you must qualify in at least one event to register. Spectators do not need to register.
Start: February 15, 6:00 PM EST
Cost: $120 for competitors
Limit: 1100 competitors
Qualifying Times: See events page.
You can get a 50% refund for cancelling until the refund deadline, after which we will no longer give refunds.
