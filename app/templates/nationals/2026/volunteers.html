Apply to be on the Volunteer Team!

Thanks for your interest! We are looking for applications for the Core Volunteer Team, Data Team, Help Desk, and Live Stream Commentators; you can read more about each role below. The application form is available here (FIX BEFORE ANNOUNCEMENT: ADD LINK). Volunteers will be accepted on a rolling basis. Please apply early for full consideration.

Signing up to be on our volunteer team is a commitment; please make sure to let us know as soon as possible if you will not be able to attend. We will need a final confirmation of your attendance no later than May 19, 2026.


Core Volunteer Team

Core Volunteer Team members will have judging, running, and scrambling assignments throughout the competition. Expect to spend about 2/3 of the competition volunteering, and the rest either on breaks or competing. Delegates accepted to the Core Volunteer Team will be able to apply to be a Stage Lead at a later date.


Data Team

Data Team members will be in charge of data entry, double checking scorecards, and printing subsequent round scorecards. Expect to spend about 2/3 of the competition volunteering, and the rest either on breaks or competing.


Help Desk

We're looking for parents and other volunteers to work around 5 hours per day at Registration, answering questions about our event and checking competitors in, and helping with some other tasks like shopping runs. This is a great job for parents who'd like to help out and keep busy during the competition!


Live Stream Team

The Live Stream Team will work with our contracted production company to provide a professional competition stream. Varying levels of involvement and time commitment are available. We are looking for volunteers with experience and interest in the following categories:

  • Stream Commentator
  • Data gathering and analysis
  • Videography
  • Stage direction

Applications for this position will close on March 15th. Please note that this role includes preparation work leading up to the competition.


Unofficial Events

We're looking for volunteers to help with other parts of the event besides the competition, including unofficial events, the WCA Booth, and other fun activities we're planning for the weekend!


Perks

Volunteer members will receive the following perks:
  • Refunded registration fee (after the competition)
  • Lunch for each day of the competition (specifics to be determined)
  • Volunteer t-shirts
  • Travel support -- one of:
    • Accommodation in a volunteer hotel room (18+ only) for 5 nights (Wednesday night through Sunday night)
    • $40 per night reimbursement for your own hotel room for 5 nights
    • $60 per night reimbursement for your own hotel room with no refunded registration (great option for parents volunteering)
    • Daily parking reimbursement (great option for local cubers)
  • One free spectator ticket for non-competing volunteers (all competitors receive one by default)
