Rubik's WCA North American Championship 2026

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA will be hosting the Rubik's WCA North American Championship 2026 on July 2-5, 2026 at the Raleigh Convention Center in Raleigh, North Carolina!

This competition will be the WCA's third North American Championship, and the nineteenth US National Championship. Each year, CubingUSA hosts a major competition bringing together hundreds of the top American speedcubers. We are pleased to welcome competitors from other North American countries, and the rest of the world, for the second North American Championship held in the US.

This competition is brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community.
