CubingUSA Nationals 2023

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA Nationals 2023 will be held July 27-30, 2023 at the David L. Lawrence Convention Center in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania!

CubingUSA Nationals 2023 will be the 16th US National Championship, and the first since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic. Each year, the competition brings together hundreds of the top American speedcubers, as well as many international competitors, for a fun weekend of solving puzzles.

Nationals is the largest and most professional speedcubing competition in the USA each year, brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community.

Key Facts

Dates: July 27-30, 2023
Location: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
Venue: David L. Lawrence Convention Center
